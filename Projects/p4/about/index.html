About

When I was a wee lad, I was a MASSIVE transformers fan. I managed to wring a LOT of figures out of my parents' wallets, and they've been laying around for quite some time.

For the sake of older, simpler times, I decided to yank out a few figures and photograph them for the sake of this project. As I was doing so, however, I was picking out a disproportionate of decepticons; TEN of them in fact. That's when I asked the question; why not?

These are ten decepti-toys I scrounged up during my fateful basement crawl. We'll look over their positives, negatives, and relation to my personal story.
