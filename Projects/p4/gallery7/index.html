Sovereign of Skullduggery: CANNONBALL

(Deluxe Class, $9.99, 2005)

  • (+)Is quite pretty, and has tampographs unlike what we usually see
  • (-)Has easily the most teeth-grindingly infuriating transformation of the the entire lot, involving big chunks having to slide around tiny panels
  • (-)His cyber-key gimmick is totally LAME

The guy I bought SOLELY because he's a repaint of a character who was of ACTUAL relevance. Fun times!

I have no idea how my kid-self was able to tolerate this guy; his transformation is actual torture. His template was a medic, so even though he's supposed to be a pirate, his accessories were a claw and hammer-head... both of which I've long since lost. Also, his cyber-key gimmick is shoulder cannons... the same as Dirt Boss in robot mode. Whee.

An assasin by trade, Cannonball specializes in toxins, subterfuge and easy kills. His inner workings are specifically tuned for silent driving and quiet steps, all while having a dedicated interface he can view for all of his killing needs. Cannonball himself is actually a loud, bombastic party man, which typically has him turn off his voice altogehter for his work.
